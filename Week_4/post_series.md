# Design a Social Media Post Series for an Event

Design a complete series of social media posts to promote an event. Create at least 5 posts that follow the same visual style and branding. The posts should look like they belong to the same campaign while sharing different information about the event.


### Things to be Included:
- A minimum of 5 social media posts.
- A consistent color palette, typography, and design style.
- Event name and branding.
- Different content for each post, such as:
- Event Announcement
- Guest/Speaker or Performer Introduction
- Schedule or Event Details
- Registration or Ticket Information
- Countdown or Final Reminder
- A clean and attractive layout for every post.


### Rules:
- All designs must be your own original work.
- Do not use pre-made templates or copy existing designs.
- Maintain the same branding and design style across all posts.
- You may use Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator only.
- Each post must be designed in 1080 × 1350 pixels (4:5 ratio).
- Submit all final designs as PNG, JPG, or PDF files.
- Submit the project files (PSD or AI) along with the final designs.


### Points: 
This task carries 100 points.
