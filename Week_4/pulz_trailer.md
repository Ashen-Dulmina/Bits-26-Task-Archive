# Create a Cinematic Trailer for PULZ'27

Create a cinematic trailer to promote PULZ'27 ICT Day of Ananda College ICT Society. The trailer should build excitement, create suspense, and encourage people to attend the event. Use creative editing, smooth transitions, visual effects, music, and motion graphics to produce a professional-looking promotional video.


### Things to be Included:
- A cinematic intro.
- The PULZ'27 logo and event branding.
- Event title and theme.
- Date, time, and venue.
- Motion graphics and visual effects.
- Background music and sound effects.
- A strong ending with a call to action (e.g., "See You at PULZ'27" or "Join the Future").


### Rules:
- The trailer must be 60–120 seconds long.
- Video resolution must be at least 1920 × 1080 (Full HD).
- You may use Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe After Effects, or DaVinci Resolve only.
- All editing, animations, and visual effects must be your own original work.
- Do not use pre-made trailer templates.
- You may use royalty-free music, sound effects, stock videos, and stock images.
- Refer to previous trailer videos from the ACICTS YouTube channel for inspiration and style reference.
- Submit the final video in MP4 format.
- Submit the project file along with the final video.


### Points: 
This task carries 100 points.
