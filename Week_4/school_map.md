# Create a School Map of Ananda College
Develop a clear and easy-to-understand map of the Ananda College premises.
You may draw the map by hand, or use simple, beginner-friendly software to make it clear and neat.


### Key Requirements:
- Clearly mark key locations such as main buildings, administrative offices, the ICT Lab, auditorium, library, canteens, and major sports grounds.
- Include a legend or key to help readers easily identify and navigate the locations.


### Guidelines & Rules:
- Plagiarism is strictly prohibited. All work must be your own. Copying maps or designs from others will lead to disqualification.
- If created digitally, upload your completed map as a PDF file or image file (e.g., JPEG/PNG) to Google Drive and provide a publicly accessible link via the submission form.
- If drawn by hand, scan or take a clear, high-resolution photograph of your map, convert it to a PDF, upload it to Google Drive, and submit the publicly accessible link.
- Your final submission should be presented clearly, showing effort and neatness in detailing the school layout.


### Points:
This Task Carries 100 points.
