# Designing a Digital Literacy Campaign for the Community
Identify a specific group with low digital literacy and create a plan to bridge the knowledge gap.


### Things to be included:-
- Identify a real-world challenge, such as senior citizens struggling with online banking or digital misinformation.
- Use questionnaires or direct interviews as a method for data collection to understand the specific needs of the target group.
- Propose a solution in the form of a structured workshop series or an easy-to-use digital resource kit.
- Only Microsoft Powerpoint or Google Slides can be used to create the presentation.
- Presentations must be delivered in person at the school ICT Lab.
- A specific date and time will be assigned to each participant; punctuality is mandatory.
- Upload your completed presentation file to Google Drive and provide a publicly accessible link via the submission form.
- Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
